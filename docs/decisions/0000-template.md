# NNNN — Short title (verb-phrase)

- **Status:** proposed | accepted | superseded by NNNN | deprecated
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Author:** name (or "Author + Claude", "Author + Codex")

## Context

What's the situation that requires a decision? Constraints, requirements, pain points. Keep it factual and short.

## Decision

What did we decide. Be specific and unambiguous.

## Alternatives considered

- **Option A** — what it was, why we didn't pick it.
- **Option B** — what it was, why we didn't pick it.

## Consequences

- **Positive:** what gets easier or better.
- **Negative:** what we're now committed to / what gets harder.
- **Reversal cost:** how expensive is it to change our mind later.

## References

Links to related ADRs, issues, PRs, external docs.
